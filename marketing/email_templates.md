## Usage Notes

- Replace `{first_name}`, `{your_name}`, and `{end_date}` with actual values
- For the discount email, create a discount code on Gumroad first and update the link
- Recommended email cadence:
  - Launch email: Send to your full list on launch day
  - Demo follow-up: Send 2-3 days after someone tries the demo
  - Discount email: Use sparingly (quarterly at most) to maintain price integrity
- A/B test subject lines — send the top performer to the remaining list after testing on 20%
